HOW IT WORKS


Food For All helps to reduce food waste by allowing you to buy great food that restaurants did not sell by the end of the day, up to 80% cheaper than its original price.

1. Search

Find the deals closest to your location.

2. Order

Buy food with the best discounts.

3. Pick-up

Get your order before restaurants close.


Food For All allows you to buy food that restaurants in your area havent sold at the end of the day that would otherwise be wasted.

The world wastes 1.6bn tonnes of food every year. Food waste accounts for about 8% of global climate pollution, more than India or Russia.

When using Food-For-All, you are not only treating yourself, you're aiding society as a whole!
