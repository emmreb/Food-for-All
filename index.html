Food For All allows people to buy food that restaurants did not sell by the end of the day that would otherwise be wasted.

Our Mission: To build social awareness regarding food waste, allowing people to buy great food from restaurants, food which would otherwise be thrown away.

Since April 2016 we have been working hard on developing our first prototype so we can confidently provide you with the best product.
